		<category name="Revolvers">
			<category name="Description">
				<comment>By Aaron0000</comment>
				<comment>Features:</comment>
				<comment>Changes the Jakobs, Maliwan, and Torgue pistols to work similarly to the Revolvers of Borderlands 1.</comment>
				<comment>High damage.</comment>
				<comment>High accuracy.</comment>
				<comment>High critical hit damage</comment>
				<comment>Low fire rate.</comment>
				<comment>High recoil.</comment>
				<comment>Consumes 2 ammo per shot (Maliwan consumes 3 per shot.)</comment>
				<comment>Double accessory is turned into a Masher accessory that only "mashes" the Revolvers, and is a normal Double accessory otherwise.</comment>
				<comment>Thanks to Koby (modified code) and Hemaxhu (original code), Jakobs pistols have an option to adjust their animations, instead of fanning the hammer.</comment>
				<category name="Notes">
					<comment>Certain special guns have been given adjustments to better fit the changes caused by revolving the Jakobs/Maliwan/Torgue pistols. Disable if needed (such as for mods that go over those items). The items in question are;</comment>
					<comment>Maggie - Adjusted to gain the same amount of effectiveness from a Masher as a typical Jakobs pistol would.</comment>
					<comment>Unforgiven - Increased the rate the accuracy returns to normal.</comment>
					<comment>Wanderlust - Adjusted to gain the same amount of effectiveness from a Masher as a typical Maliwan pistol would.</comment>
					<comment>E-Tech Pistols - Adjusted ammo consumption for revolvers in consideration of the low fire rate and lack of an ability to crit.</comment>
				</category>
				<category name="List of fixes for other mods">
					<comment>If you want to use the standalone version of mashers (that "mashes" all pistols, instead of just Jakobs/Maliwan/Torgue), just activate Mashers after this mod.</comment>
					<comment>If using my Plasma Pistols mod, there is a fix for the standard Maliwan e-tech pistols (or Jakobs and Torgue, if you have the mod for it) and the Wanderlust at the bottom.</comment>
					<comment>If using my Defiler mod, there is a fix for it at the bottom.</comment>
					<comment>If using my Smasher mod, there is a fix for it at the bottom (to reduce the ammo cost and make it mash properly).</comment>
					<comment>If using my 88 Fragnum mod, there is a fix for it at the bottom (to reduce the ammo cost and make it mash properly).</comment>
					<comment>If using the Triple from The Campin' Dutchpack mod, there is a fix for each version of it at the bottom.</comment>
					<comment>If using my Violator mod, there is a fix for it at the bottom (to reduce the ammo cost and make it mash properly).</comment>
					<comment>If using the Salvation from The Campin' Dutchpack mod, there is a fix for its damage (to compensate for the lack of critting) at the bottom.</comment>
					<comment>If using the Painbringer from The Campin' Dutchpack mod, there is a fix for it at the bottom.</comment>
				</category>
